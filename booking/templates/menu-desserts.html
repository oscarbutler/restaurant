Desserts

Chocolate Fondant:
A decadent and gooey chocolate cake with a molten center. Served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream and a drizzle of raspberry coulis.
Crème Brûlée:
A classic French dessert featuring vanilla custard with a perfectly caramelized sugar crust. Garnished with fresh berries.
Tiramisu:
Layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, dusted with cocoa powder and served with a side of chocolate sauce.
Lemon Soufflé:
A light and fluffy lemon-flavored soufflé served with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.
Panna Cotta with Berry Compote:
Smooth vanilla-infused panna cotta topped with a vibrant mixed berry compote and a mint garnish.
White Chocolate Raspberry Cheesecake:
Creamy white chocolate cheesecake with a raspberry swirl, served on a buttery graham cracker crust.
Hazelnut Praline Mousse:
Velvety hazelnut mousse layered with a crunchy praline base, topped with whipped cream and caramelized hazelnuts.
Poached Pear in Red Wine:
Pears poached in red wine and spices, served with a drizzle of reduced poaching liquid and a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
Opera Cake:
A layered almond sponge cake with coffee buttercream and chocolate ganache. Served with a raspberry coulis.
Mango Sorbet with Prosecco:
Refreshing mango sorbet served in a chilled glass, topped with a splash of Prosecco and a mint sprig.
