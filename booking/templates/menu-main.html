Pan-seared duck breast with a luscious cherry port wine sauce. Accompanied by wild rice pilaf and sautéed haricot verts.

Delicate sea bass fillet pan-seared to a golden brown and served with a velvety lemon beurre blanc sauce. Paired with asparagus tips and truffle-infused mashed potatoes.
